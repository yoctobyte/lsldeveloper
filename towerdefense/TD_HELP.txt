===================================
  TT TOWER DEFENCE  — Player Guide
===================================

GOAL
Stop waves of monster walkers from crossing the board.
Each walker that reaches the far end costs you 1 Life.
Reach 0 Lives → Game Over.

---
RESOURCES
  Funds  — earned passively (1/second). Buy & upgrade towers.
  Energy — generated by Energy Plants; consumed by tower shots.
  Karma  — consumed by every shot. Value your ammo.
  Lives  — start at 10. Gain 1 per completed level.

---
BUILDING TOWERS
1. Touch the board while a game is in progress.
2. The engine finds the nearest valid grid square.
3. Choose "Place Tower" to drop a foundation.
4. Touch the new tower to pick its type:

  ENERGY PLANT  — Generates Energy each tick. Upgrade for more output.
  DEFENSE TOWER — Restores Lives over time.
  BALLISTIC     — Rapid fire. Costs 1 Energy + 1 Karma per shot.
  SNIPER        — High damage, ignores shields. Costs 5 Energy per shot.
  FOG           — Slows walkers in area. Constant Energy drain.
  TOXIC         — Damage-over-time cloud. Low Energy cost.
  TESLA         — Chain-lightning burst. High Karma cost.
  WALL          — Blocks the path; walkers must smash through.

---
UPGRADING
Touch any built tower → "Upgrade" (cost doubles each time, base 500 funds).
Each upgrade increases range, fire rate, and damage.

---
SELLING
Touch a tower → "Sell" — returns 10% of upgrade investment.

---
TIPS
• Place Energy Plants early — every shot drains energy.
• Walls create chokepoints; pair them with Ballistic towers.
• Sniper towers ignore the walker's shield stat.
• Fog + Toxic stacks slow AND damage simultaneously.
• If Karma runs out, all attack towers stop firing.

---
LEVELS (7 defined, repeats after)
  1 — Training: slow Ants (30 HP)
  2 — Fields: more Ants, mixed tower practice
  3 — Choke Point: armored Scorpions (150 HP)
  4 — Desert: fast Flies (80 HP, 0.8x speed)
  5 — Valley: mixed wave, 30 walkers
  6 — Industrial: Drones, energy management required
  7 — Fortress: armored Tanks (500 HP) — use Snipers
